My Logo Story

This was made by me on Adobe Photoshop. All the lines and dots are from my name, 아라.

Most Korean names are written in Chinese characters. So, the sound and meaning are different can it can be written in both Korean and Chinese characters.

However, my name is made in pure Korean and it has only one form. Therefore, I made my logo by transforming my Korean name into the form of "Hun-min-jeong-um", which is the origin of Korean.

아라 means "Ocean".
